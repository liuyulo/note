16 Refute Hume's Skepticism

Hume's Argument without PUN

Hume: Reliability of Induction is Circular
  • induction was reliable in the past
  • ∴ by induction, induction will be reliable in the future
    (Hume: see? circular)

Proof. suppose induction argument is not circular, then

  1. counterinduction was unreliable in the past
  2. ∴ by counterinduction, counterinduction will be reliable in the future
    (Sober: workn't ∴ induction argument is circular)
Hume again
  • can't show induction is reliable using deductive arguments (using the past)
  • can't show induction is reliable using inductive argument (circular)
  • ∴ can't show induction is reliable
  • ∴ can't rationally justify induction
    (Hume: didn't use PUN)
