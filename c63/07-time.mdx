## Hardness & Completeness

<details class="definition" open>
<summary>$A\le_pB\iff\exists$ computable $f:\Sigma^*\to\Sigma^*$ st</summary>

for any $w\in\Sigma^*$
$$
w\in A\iff f(w)\in B
$$
and $f$ computes in polynomial time
</details>

<details class="definition" open>
<summary>$L\sube\Sigma^*$</summary>
- is NPH iff $\forall A\in NP\enspace A\le_p L$
- is NPC iff $L\in NP$ and NPH
</details>

## NP

<details class="definition" open>
<summary>verifier </summary>
a **verifier** $V$ for $A\sube\Sigma^*$ is a TM st
$$
A=\{w:\exists c\enspace V\textsf{ accepts }\langle w,c\rangle\}
$$

where $c$ is the **certificate**

- time of verifier depends only on $|w|$
</details>

<details class="definition" open>
<summary>NP</summary>
$NP$ is the class of all languages recognisable by a polytime verifier

to show language $L\in NP$ 

1. show $L$ is a decision problem
2. for any $w\in L$, provide a certificate $c$ of $w$
3. show $|c|$ is polysize (wrt $|w|$)
4. provide a verifier $V$ (decider on $\langle w,c\rangle$)
5. show $V$ runs in polytime
</details>

<details class="definition" open>
<summary>coNP</summary>

$$
L\in \text{coNP}\iff\overline L\in NP
$$
</details>

## P

<details class="definition" open>
<summary>time complexity class</summary>
for any $t:\mathbb N\to\mathbb R^+$, define

$$
\text{TIME}(t)
$$
to be class of all languages decidable by an $\mathcal O(t)$ TM
</details>

<details class="definition" open>
<summary>class P</summary>
let $P$ be the class of all languages decidable by a DSTM

$$
P=\bigcup_k\text{TIME}(n^k)
$$
</details>
