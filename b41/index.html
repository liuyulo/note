Extreme Value Theorem

for f:U\sub\R^n\to\R

  • D_r(a)=\{x\in\R^n:||x-a||<r\}\sub\R^n is a open ball

  • U is open \iff\partial U=\varnothing, else closed

  • U is bounded \iff\exist M\enspace U\sube D_M(0) for sufficiently large M

  • U is compact \iff U is closed and bounded
