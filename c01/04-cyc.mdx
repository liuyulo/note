title: Cyclic Group
---

import Tikz from '../../components/Tikz.astro'

<details class="definition" open>
<summary>group generator $a\in G$</summary>
$$
\langle a\rangle=\{a^n:n\in\mathbb Z\}\le G
$$

$G$ is cyclic iff $\langle a\rangle=G$

<details>
<summary>**Proof.** $\langle a\rangle\le G$</summary>
- $a\in\langle a\rangle\implies\langle a\rangle$ nonempty
- for any $a^n,a^m\in\langle a\rangle$
  - $a^n(a^m)^{-1}=a^{n-m}\in\langle a\rangle$
- $\therefore\langle a\rangle\le G$ 
</details>

- cyclic groups are abelian
</details>



<details class="theorem" open>
<summary>for a group $G$ and $a\in G$</summary>

case $|a|=n<\infty$

- $a^i=a^j\iff n|(i-j)$

 case $|a|=\infty$

- $a^i=a^j\iff i=j$

</details>
<details class="theorem" open>
<summary>for any $a,b\in G$</summary>
$|a|=|\langle a\rangle|$

- $a^k=e$ iff $|a|$ divides $k$
- $|G|<\infty$ and $ab=ba\implies |ab|$ divides $|a||b|$
</details>

<details class="example" open>
<summary>infinite group $G=SL(2,\mathbb R)$</summary>
let 
$$
\begin{aligned}
&&A&=\begin{pmatrix}0&-1\\1&0\\\end{pmatrix}\quad B=\begin{pmatrix}0&1\\-1&-1\\\end{pmatrix}\in G\\
\therefore&&AB&=\begin{pmatrix}1&0\\0&1\\\end{pmatrix}\quad(AB)^n=\begin{pmatrix}1&n\\0&1\\\end{pmatrix}
\end{aligned}
$$

$\therefore |A|=4$ and $|B|=3$, but $|AB|=\infty$
</details>

<details class="theorem" open>
<summary>let $|G|=n$ and $d=\gcd(n,k)$</summary>
$$
\langle a^k\rangle=\langle a^d\rangle\quad|a^k|=n/d
$$

- $\therefore$ if $|a|=n$, then $\langle a\rangle=\langle a^k\rangle\iff \gcd(n,k)=1$
</details>

<details class="theorem" open>
<summary>fundamental theorem of cyclic group</summary>

let any cyclic group $\langle a\rangle$ with $|a|<\infty$

- for any $G\le\langle a\rangle$, $G$ is cyclic and $|G|$ divides $|a|$
- for any divisor $d$ of $|a|$, the only subgroup with order $d$ is $\langle a^{n/d}\rangle$
</details>
