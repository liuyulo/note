## Definitions

<summary>diagonal of $G$</summary>
$$
\{(g,g):g\in G\}\le G\oplus G
$$
</details>

## External Direct Product

<details class="definition" open>
<summary>external direct product</summary>
let $(G_1,\cdots,G_n)$ be groups, the external direct product is the group

$$
G=\prod_{i=1}^nG_i=G_1\oplus\cdots\oplus G_n=\{(g_1,\cdots,g_n):g_i\in G_i\}
$$

where the group operation of $G$ is componentwise

- i.e. $(a_1,\cdots,a_n)(b_1,\cdots,b_n)=(a_1b_1,\cdots,a_nb_n)$
</details>

<details class="theorem" open>
<summary>let $G_i$ be finite groups</summary>
- $\left|\prod G_i\right|=\prod|G_i|$
- $|(a_1,\cdots,a_n)|=\operatorname{lcm}(|a_1|,\cdots,|a_n|)$
- if $G_1$ and $G_2$ are cyclic
  - then $G_1\oplus G_2$ cyclic $\iff|G_1|$ and $|G_2|$ coprime
- $U(pq)\cong U(p)\oplus U(q)$ for coprime $p,q$
</details>
