# Constituent
> *syntax*: study of sentence structure

- constituents = (syntatic) *phrases*
    - used to form *grammatical claues* (sentences)
- predicate: **everything** except subject
# Constituency Tests
## Replacement Test
- \[*the quick brown fox*\] jumps over the lazy dog
- \[*it*\] jumps over the lazy dog

## Stand-Alone Test
- Q: the quick brown fox jumps over <abbr title="the lazy dog"><em>what</em></abbr>?
- A: \[*the lazy dog*\]

## Movement Test
- I will go \[*tomorrow*\]
- \[*tomorrow*\], I will go

### Clefting
- she received \[*a bouquet of flowers*\]
- **it was** \[*a bouquet of flowers*\] **that** she received
