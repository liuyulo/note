Constituent

syntax: study of sentence structure

  • constituents = (syntatic) phrases
    • used to form grammatical claues (sentences)
  • predicate: everything except subject # Constituency Tests ## Replacement Test
  • [the quick brown fox] jumps over the lazy dog
  • [it] jumps over the lazy dog

Stand-Alone Test

  • Q: the quick brown fox jumps over what?
  • A: [the lazy dog]

Movement Test

  • I will go [tomorrow]
  • [tomorrow], I will go

Clefting

  • she received [a bouquet of flowers]
  • it was [a bouquet of flowers] that she received
